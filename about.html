My Story

I am an instructional technology specialist with a background in computer information systems and business management. I design effective, accessible e-learning solutions that connect people with complex tools and ideas. My passion lies at the intersection of education, psychology, and technology and my goal is creating meaningful learning experiences that drive measurable outcomes.

My experience is rooted in the corporate sector, where I have partnered with cross-functional teams to build onboarding programs, enablement initiatives, and scalable learning strategies. I take a learner-first approach, blending technical fluency with instructional design best practices to boost engagement, retention, and confidence.

I am currently completing my Master of Science in Instructional Technology at the University of North Carolina Wilmington and will graduate in December 2026.
